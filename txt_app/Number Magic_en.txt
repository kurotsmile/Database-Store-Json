Use your logical brain to arrange the decoding of matrices in each stage of the game to help improve your thinking. With extremely simple gameplay suitable for you to entertain in the relaxation time

How to play:
You control each cluster of numbers around the black control button in the rows and columns in the numerical matrices, the numbers will be rotated clockwise, you use your intelligence to find sets out the rules for arranging numbers in small to large numbers. When finished you will be the winner
