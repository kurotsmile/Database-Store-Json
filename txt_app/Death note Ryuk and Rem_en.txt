There are always people stalking human life. The grim reapers are the characters this game is talking about!

Do you really understand all about the god of death and you know that those gods love to eat our apples

Play the role of the god of death and go eat apples with a novel and heterogeneous gameplay that will make you happy. Get higher scores to conquer the game and other players

The way to play is simple, but getting points is very difficult for you. You have to control two characters at the same time using the dexterity between your left and right brain hemispheres to help the two grim reapers not collide with objects.
