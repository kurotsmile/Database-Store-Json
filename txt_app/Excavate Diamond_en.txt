Transform into a miner to mine precious diamonds

Fill your diamond collection with a variety of diamonds not only diamonds but also other rare stones such as ruby, quartz, and bluestone

The rules of the game are very simple, just move and change the diamonds so that all 3 types of the same color can be collected into your collection and you will have a score.

The game has built-in leaderboards so you can compete for scores with your friends and people around the world

Simple gameplay, eye-catching collection of diamonds will help you have relaxing moments after every working hour
