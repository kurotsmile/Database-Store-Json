Triple Items is a simple but challenging puzzle game. The goal of the game is to match 3 identical items together to create a new item. The new item will have a greater power than the old item.

To play Triple Items, you need to move the items around the game board by tapping on them. When you have found 3 identical items, they will automatically merge together.

The number of points you receive will depend on the power of the new item. The stronger the item, the more points you will receive.

You will have 30 seconds to merge as many items as possible. The player with the most points at the end of the time will win the game.

Triple Items is a great game for fun and brain training. Try it today!
