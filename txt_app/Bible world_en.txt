This is an application to help you understand more about God through biblical historical documents

Make yourself a good habit of reading a scripture and praying every night, which will help you improve your soul to live through bible verses.

In addition, the application also provides images to describe each corresponding biblical passage to help people more intuitively understand the plot. And lots of warm, inspiring voices to make this bible app special

You can also save book chapters to read when connected to the network, the corresponding photo and audio collection will be saved with that chapter.
