The game helps you improve your agility with loads of visual icons. Also you can play this game with two player mode

How to play 1 person:
You must choose the same pair of pictures in the list of hidden boxes, each level will have different challenges. Ask players to pass challenges in a certain amount of time

How to play 2 people (Fighting)
Similar to playing one, the game will split two levels and different saints for players to quickly test their skills. The person who completes the challenge will be the first winner
