The chessboard is a place to show your intelligence and calculation skills. Understanding the opponent's psychology and predicting the way is what you need to pay attention to when participating in this game

You will be the winner when you know the rules of the game, but don't forget that you have to be very careful with your pieces.

This game plays like checkers, but it is difficult to get points when you block the opponent's two pieces. This way of playing makes it more interesting and special than traditional playstyles. You can play with friends in turn-based mode or play with artificial intelligence in AI mode
