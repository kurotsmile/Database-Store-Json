Line 98 Fruta is an intellectual game that is no longer strange to the office world. How to play is simple, but no less interesting Line 98 has attracted a large number of players to participate

One of the things that makes Line 98 Fruta attractive is the very simple way to play, you move one ball per turn, and each time you move one ball a new one appears. The goal is to put 5 balls of the same color together to make them disappear.

If the table is full and you can't move any ball, you lose.

Line 98 Fruta is a funny game to enjoy yourself or for your friend
