Rowan is a talented pilot trained to destroy missiles that pursue their targets

Unfortunately his aircraft radar is broken and visibility is poor, it's very dangerous and he needs your help.

Let's join forces with him to destroy the missiles that follow his plane

The gameplay is very simple, just press the two control buttons at the bottom of the screen to change the flight trajectory of the aircraft. Cutting the tail and causing the rockets to collide and explode will help you score

In particular, the game also supports many types of gamepads
